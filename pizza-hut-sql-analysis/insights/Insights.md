## Key Insights

1. Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of pizza types.
   A small subset of pizzas contributes a disproportionately large share
   of total revenue.

2. Pizza size has a strong impact on revenue generation.
   Larger-sized pizzas generate higher revenue compared to smaller sizes.

3. Order volume varies significantly by time of day.
   Certain hours consistently experience higher order frequency,
   indicating peak demand periods.

4. Pizza categories perform differently in terms of quantity and revenue.
   Some categories are ordered more frequently, while others generate
   higher revenue due to pricing.

5. Daily order volume remains relatively stable with periodic spikes,
   suggesting predictable demand with occasional high-activity days.

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus promotions and marketing on high-revenue pizza types and sizes.
2. Allocate staffing and operational resources during peak ordering hours.
3. Review low-performing pizza types for optimization or removal.
4. Introduce bundled offers around top-performing categories to
   maximize revenue per order.
